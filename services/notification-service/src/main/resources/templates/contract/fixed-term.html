3. JOB TITLE AND DESCRIPTION

Job Title: Position Title
Department: Department

4. REMUNERATION

Monthly Salary: R 25,000.00
Currency: ZAR
Payment Frequency: Monthly, on or before the last business day of each month
Payment Method: Electronic transfer to the Employee's nominated bank account

5. WORKING HOURS

The Employee's normal working hours shall be 08:00 - 17:00, Monday to Friday, not exceeding 45 hours per week in accordance with Section 9 of the BCEA.
Overtime shall be compensated in accordance with Section 10 of the BCEA.

6. LEAVE ENTITLEMENT

6.1 Annual Leave: The Employee shall be entitled to 15 working days (or 21 consecutive days) of paid annual leave per annual leave cycle, pro-rated for the duration of the contract, in accordance with Section 20 of the BCEA.

6.2 Sick Leave: The Employee shall be entitled to paid sick leave in accordance with Section 22 of the BCEA, pro-rated for the contract period.

6.3 Family Responsibility Leave: 3 days per annual leave cycle, in accordance with Section 27 of the BCEA.

6.4 Maternity Leave: 4 consecutive months, in accordance with Section 25 of the BCEA.

7. RENEWAL AND EXTENSION

7.1 This contract may be renewed or extended by mutual written agreement of both parties.

7.2 In accordance with Section 198B of the LRA, if this contract is renewed or extended beyond 3 months (and the Employee earns below the BCEA earnings threshold), the Employee may be deemed to be a permanent employee unless the fixed-term nature of the contract is justified.

7.3 The Employer shall provide the Employee with reasonable notice of whether the contract will be renewed at least 1 month before the end date.

8. EARLY TERMINATION

8.1 Either party may terminate this contract before its natural expiry by giving 4 (four) weeks' written notice, or payment in lieu of notice.

8.2 The Employer may summarily dismiss the Employee for serious misconduct in accordance with the provisions of the LRA.

8.3 Upon early termination, the Employee shall be entitled to payment for leave not yet taken, calculated on a pro-rata basis.

9. DEDUCTIONS

Deductions from remuneration shall only be made in accordance with Section 34 of the BCEA, including statutory deductions (PAYE, UIF, SDL) and any deductions agreed to in writing.

10. CONFIDENTIALITY

The Employee agrees not to disclose any confidential information relating to the Employer's business, clients, or affairs during or after the term of this contract, except as required by law.

11. DISPUTE RESOLUTION

Any dispute arising from this contract shall be referred to the CCMA in accordance with the Labour Relations Act 66 of 1995.

12. GENERAL

12.1 This contract constitutes the entire agreement between the parties.

12.2 Any amendments must be in writing and signed by both parties.

12.3 This contract is governed by the laws of the Republic of South Africa.
